Physical Education

The Physical Education department at MACE is committed to providing students with an opportunity to developing their extracurricular skills by focusing on sports such as cricket, football, basketball etc. The college conducts the Inter Collegiate fest, MAESTRO, wherein various sports competitions are conducted and the winners awarded cash prizes every year.

Facilities

Some of the facilities available in the campus are

  • Cricket Net Court
  • Football Court
  • Volleyball Court

The necessary sports gears required for the proper conduct of various games like cricket, football, basketball, table tennis, throw ball, soft ball, volleyball, chess and caroms are available.
