NSS

The National Service Scheme wing at MACE is responsible for a number of college activities, all performed as a part of community service. Awareness classes, debates, lectures and talks on various important topics are regularly conducted. Free Medical Camps, checkups and free clinical tests are also a regular show here at MACE. We have had many blood donation camps too. The pipe compost, a solution to waste disposal, has been installed at MACE and is a grand success. Planting of seedlings throughout the campus, colony road and in the adopted village have been carried out as a reply to “the global warming effect as part of ‘the Tree Programme”.
