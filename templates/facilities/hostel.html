MACE students enjoy a vibrant and dynamic living and learning environment on our stunning residential campus. Our residential community celebrates and promotes diversity, fosters respect and leadership skills and facilitates a wide variety of experiences and activities that support the development of well rounded individuals. MACE houses architecturally elegant and well organized separate hostel facilities for boys and girls at affordable costs. The Boys Hostel can accommodate approximately 150 students and the Girls Hostel can house almost 100 students. Spacious study halls and mess halls are highlights of the hostel. Round the clock high security is provided to safeguard the students from any unpleasantness and guarantee a safe stay.
