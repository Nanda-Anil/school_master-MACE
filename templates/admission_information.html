Eligibility

Minimum of 50% marks in Part III for B.A, B.Sc & B.Com graduates and 50% aggregate marks (for all semesters/all parts) for all other graduates. University of Kerala norms applies in the case of OBC & SC/ST candidates. Candidates seeking admission under this quota should attach copies of Caste Certiﬁcate along with the application form.

How to Apply

  • Candidates are required to apply online by filling up the application carefully.
  • An amount of Rs.500/- should also be paid online towards the application fee.
  • Attach a copy of CAT, CMAT or K-MAT (Kerala), score card.

Selection Criteria

Selection of candidates to MBA Programme is based on eligibility criteria as ﬁxed by the University of kerala as above and the minimum score obtained in Management Aptitude Test (CAT, C-MAT and K-MAT/(Kerala), as prescribed by admission regulatory committee and the Group Discussion and Personal Interview conducted by the Institute.
CAT, CMAT or K-MAT (Kerala) score carries a weightage of 80%. Group Discussion & Personal Interview carry a weightage of 10% each. Final Selection is based on the composite score calculated from CAT, C-MAT or K-MAT(Kerala) Score, Group Discussion and Personal Interview.
