PTA

MACE has an efficient Parents-Teachers Association (PTA) system. The PTA consists of the co-ordinating staff members along with elected guardians of two or more (as decided by the management council) students as members. The PTA is a common forum for regular interaction between teachers and parents to discuss needs and status of students, academic-wise and otherwise. Overall improvement of the students is the primary focus here. All concerns of the parents are dealt by the PTA. The PTA also helps in fostering a good relationship and thereby an incredible working environment between staff, students and the respective guardians. It gives special care and attention to the needs of students from socially economic background. The PTA works in co-ordination with the CGPU for the welfare of the students.
