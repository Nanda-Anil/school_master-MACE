Counselling Centre

    Home Facilities

The main aim of the Counselling centre is to provide appropriate counselling to every student in a friendly environment, keeping in mind the needs of the student community. The student friendly counselling is handled with utmost care to help students to overcome their fears and problems. Sensitive issues that disturb the student and hinders his/her studies are addressed and solved. Students can seek the support of the counsellors for their social and emotional problems and any other difficulties, as and when required. The Counselling center works in close management with the departments and parents, thus ensuring smooth functioning of all its activities.
